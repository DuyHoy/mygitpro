TFT Origins List

Find a list of all the Origins in Teamfight Tactics with their corresponding Bonuses and Champions.

Bonus
Units
Abomination
When 3 allied champions have died, the Monstrosity will rise from its grave. The Monstrosity receives bonus Health and Attack Damage based on allied Abomination champions' star levels. The 3 Abomination champions nearest the grave will also grant the Monstrosity a random copy of one of their items when combat starts.
